Although it is estimated that there are at least 24,000 people with autism in Singapore, including 18,000 over 19 years old, there are only 2 autism-focused Day Activity Centre in Singapore, they are Eden Centre for Adults and St. Andrew Autism Centre. In particular, Eden Centre for Adults is a Day Activity Centre which serves people with autism from 18 to 55 years old.

There is a significant demand for a second Eden Centre for Adults.

Your donations will be pooled and become part of the reserve for establishing the second Eden Centre for Adults.

The mission of the AAS is to initiate, organise and support provision of a broad spectrum of services for people with autism in Singapore. Such services to encompass education, vocational and life skills training and residential care.

Under the flagship of AAS, Eden Children's Centre (Clementi and Simei), Eden School (Bukit Batok) and Eden Centre for Adults (Hougang) provide education, training and welfare for people with autism.

All of these institutions provide delicate care and quality education and training for people with autism so as to explore and maximize their potential and talents.

For more information about the Autism Association (Singapore), please visit www.autismlinks.org.sg.
